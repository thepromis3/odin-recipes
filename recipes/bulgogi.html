Bulgogi Recipe

Description

One of my favorite Korean dishes when I eat out! It's a mix of meat and vegetables flavored with spicy Korean sauces. There's no way that you don't get seconds of white rice when you're devouring this dish.

If you're interested, definitely check out the recipe by clicking on the link above!

Ingredients

  • Thinly sliced beef
  • Onions
  • Cabbage
  • Chives
  • Carrots
  • Shimeji Mushrooms
  • A (Liquidy Sauce)
    • Soy sauce
    • Sugar
    • Miso
    • Mirin
    • Sake
    • Ginger
    • Garlic
  • B (Thicc Sauce)
    • Gochujan
    • Toubanjan
    • Sesame Seed
    • Honey
    • Sesame oil

Steps

  1. Mix the liquidy sauce (A) together, and boil it for 3 minutes.
  2. Mix the thicc sauce (B) and let it chill for now.
  3. Cut the beef and onions, and pour "A" over it. Let is chill in the fridge for an hour.
  4. Cut the rest of the vegetables into 2 inches.
  5. Take #3 out of the fridge and start sauteeing it, and after a few minutes, put 2 tablespoons of "B" on it.
  6. Add the rest of the vegetables, and add as much of "B" as you want. Should be ready to eat now!
