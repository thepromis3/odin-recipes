Curry Recipe

Description

This dish is a go-to for all Japanese people. It's super easy to make, the ingredients are straightforward, and it's delicious. In terms of taste, it's like a milder version of Indian curry, and you can put any type of meat (chicken and beef most common), and all types of vegetables. It can be had with rice, noodles, or bread, and at any time in the day.

If you're interested, definitely check out the recipe by clicking on the link above!

Ingredients

  • Curry paste
  • Onions
  • Carrots
  • Potatoes
  • Beef
  • Water
  • Rice
  • Tomatoes
  • Yogurt
  • Apples
  • Turmeric
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Marinate beef in Turmeric and Yogurt for 10 minutes.
  2. Use olive oil to sautee the onions, carrots, potatoes, and beef in that order, until beef is cooked.
  3. Pour water over #2 until most of it is covered and boil.
  4. Start making the rice.
  5. Once #3 comes to a boil, add tomatoes and apples, and lastly the curry paste.
  6. Let is simmer for another 10 minutes.
  7. Pour curry over rice and enjoy!
