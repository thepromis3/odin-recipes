Shogayaki Recipe

Description

Thinly sliced pork, flavored with gingery soy sauce. It goes well with thinly sliced cabbage as well as hot white rice. It doesn't take too long to make, and you get full from it, so a great option for big families too.

Ingredients

  • Soy sauce
